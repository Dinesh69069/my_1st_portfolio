Example of an Ordered List

Here’s a basic ordered list of steps for baking a cake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder).
  3. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  4. Pour the mixture into a baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.

  1. First item
